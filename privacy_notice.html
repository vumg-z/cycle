PRIVACY NOTICE

In accordance with the regulations set forth by the "Federal Law on the Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties" (Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de Particulares - LEY) and the "Regulation of the Federal Law on the Protection of Personal Data Held by Private Parties" (REGLAMENTO), as well as the specifications and parameters provided in the Privacy Notice guidelines published in the Official Gazette of the Federation, Access to Information, and Personal Data Protection (INAI), the company known as "REDCYCLE," hereinafter referred to as "REDCYCLE" (THE RESPONSIBLE PARTY), makes available to the general public this Comprehensive Privacy Notice for the Protection of Personal Data (Privacy Notice).

Controller of Personal Data.
The entity responsible for the collection, handling, and safeguarding of information will be "REDCYCLE." The information will be used based on the processing and purposes outlined in this Privacy Notice. For the Controller, the legitimate processing of your data is a top priority. This Privacy Notice complements any other simplified privacy notices that our organization may provide and is supplementary in all matters not expressly addressed in such notices.

Personal Data Collected.
"REDCYCLE" will collect the following data from its customers, suppliers, and service providers: name, nationality, place of birth, marital status, age, residential and work addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, taxpayer identification number (RFC), bank details, account statements, asset-related information, copy of official identification, handwritten signature, photographs through copies of official identifications, and static images. Additionally, "REDCYCLE" will request the following data from its employees and individuals with whom it has a direct employment relationship: name, date of birth, place of birth, home address, phone number, email address, unique population registration code (CURP), taxpayer identification number (RFC), marital status, criminal history, birth certificate, proof of address, information related to the property in which they reside, military record, documents related to their health status, recommendation letters, educational background, educational achievements, degree or certificate attesting to their educational level, handwritten signature, fingerprint, Social Security number, INFONAVIT credit number, driver's license, and photographs through copies of official identifications and static images. Any information provided regarding third-party personal data as reference or contact elements will be subject to "REDCYCLE's" information protection measures. "REDCYCLE" undertakes not to disclose or transmit such information, subject to this privacy notice, and those who provide such information will not be exempt from responsibility.

Purpose of Information Use.
Through this Privacy Notice, "REDCYCLE" declares that the personal data requested and collected will serve the following purposes: a) entering into contracts or agreements, b) fulfilling existing obligations with the data subject, c) assessing financial and asset-related information, d) identifying the data subject, e) providing information about quotations, products, and services we offer, as well as following up on them, f) making our advertising available to individuals, g) conducting quality control surveys, h) evaluating prospects as customers and/or service providers and/or suppliers, i) compiling the prospect's and/or customer's and/or service provider's and/or supplier's file, j) for communication purposes with the data subject, k) registering them as a customer and/or supplier, l) creating a profile of the data subject for the purposes stated herein, and m) transferring their data to other companies within the corporate group, commercial partners, and/or service providers when required. The personal data requested by "REDCYCLE" from its employees will serve the following purposes:

I. Entering into contracts or agreements.

II. Identifying the job candidate.

III. Evaluating the job candidate.

IV. Compiling the job candidate's or employee's file.

V. For future reference and background checks.

VI. To comply with fiscal, labor, and/or social security obligations.


Confidentiality and Limitations.
"REDCYCLE" guarantees strict confidentiality and privacy of information through administrative, technical, and physical security measures. These measures are in line with the law and its regulations and other applicable standards. Additionally, "REDCYCLE" manages and safeguards the personal data obtained through databases, which are solely and exclusively managed by designated individuals, restricting access to unauthorized persons. Under no circumstances will personal data be sold, rented, alienated, shared, transmitted, or transferred without the data subject's consent. Databases are protected at all times by computer security systems to restrict access or processing of information by external parties and/or unauthorized third parties. If you wish to limit the use or disclosure of personal data in relation to one or more of the data processing purposes, you must submit the respective request following the procedure outlined in the ARCO Rights module of this Privacy Notice.

Use of Cookies.
"REDCYCLE's" website uses cookies (text files automatically downloaded and stored on the electronic device used to access it) to recognize regular users and track their usage behavior on our website. Cookies do not harm your files or allow anyone to access your personal data. It simply allows us to personalize information to provide better service. Please note that contact information may be inserted into the contact section of our website. Cookies are used to obtain information based on the browser and operating system.

Transfer of Personal Data.
"REDCYCLE" will not transfer the personal data of our customers, suppliers, and service providers, except when the involvement of a third party is necessary to fulfill the legal relationship that gave rise to the processing of personal data. In such cases, that third party will be responsible, as required by law or competent authority. Likewise, with regard to our employees, information will not be shared unless required by law or requested by a competent authority.

ARCO Rights (Access, Rectification, Cancellation, and Opposition).
The data subject, either personally or through their duly accredited legal representative, may initiate the ARCO Rights procedure to demand proper attention to their personal data and request the termination of the use of their information. We commit to conducting the procedure in accordance with the provisions of the law and its regulations. The procedure can be carried out in writing at the address indicated in this Privacy Notice in the section on addressing questions and complaints or by email at the provided address. The ARCO Rights procedure is explained in detail in the ARCO Rights module available in this Privacy Notice or in physical form at our offices and can be requested by the data subject, whether in physical or electronic form, as long as they have a relationship with "REDCYCLE." Please be informed that after exhausting all available avenues that we offer and not receiving a satisfactory response, you may initiate a Rights Protection Procedure with the "National Institute for Access to Information and Data Protection" (INAI), whose electronic link is www.ifai.gob.mx and/or www.inai.gob.mx, within 15 days following the receipt or lack of response to your request.

Expression of Consent.
For "REDCYCLE" to process your personal data, you must grant us explicit and/or implicit consent, which will be given as follows: Express Consent: - As established by section 8 of the Law, it will be considered that you have given your explicit consent for the processing of your personal data when you express your will verbally, in writing, electronically, optically, or by any other technology. "REDCYCLE" will make this Privacy Notice available to you through its website and/or by email and/or in physical form at the start of any relationship or upon request in person at the address provided in the "Addressing Questions and Complaints" section of this Privacy Notice, with the primary purpose of informing you of its contents and allowing you to grant or deny your consent. When data is obtained directly from the data subject, "REDCYCLE" will make this Privacy Notice available to you before collecting personal data in a clear and convincing manner through the formats and means specified in this Privacy Notice. Tacit Consent: - In accordance with Article 8 of the Law, the content of this Privacy Notice and everything related to it will be made available to the data subject through any of the means established in this Privacy Notice. If you do not expressly refuse the processing of your personal data within a period of 5 (five) calendar days from the date "REDCYCLE" makes this Privacy Notice or its modifications available to you, it will be understood that you have given your tacit consent for us to process your personal data based on the processing purposes set out in this Privacy Notice. Retroactive Acceptance: - For all our customers, users, service providers, suppliers, and personal data subjects who provided personal data before the publication of this privacy notice, this Privacy Notice will be made available to you in accordance with the preceding sections, so that you can grant or deny your consent for us to continue or not to continue processing your personal data based on the processing purposes set out in this Privacy Notice. It is understood that, in the absence of any opposition to the content within 5 (five) natural days from the date it is made available to you, you tacitly grant your consent for us to process your personal data based on this Privacy Notice.

Revocation of Consent.
The revocation of consent for information handling will proceed when requested and when you prove to be the data subject and it is legally viable to proceed with it. If there is a current contractual relationship or a pending obligation to fulfill, the revocation will be subject to the contract that gave rise to the legal relationship, in accordance with the provisions of sections 16, subsection II of the Law, 14, 21, 30, 40, 41, and 42 of the Regulation. The revocation of your consent can be carried out after 5 (five) natural days have passed from the date you were given the opportunity to express your refusal to data processing. You can request the revocation at the address provided in the "Addressing Questions and Complaints" section by submitting a written request that expressly mentions your name and the following statement: "Through this written document, I (data subject's name) request that the legal entity 'REDCYCLE' revoke my consent for the processing of my personal data." Include the reason for the revocation, the approximate date on which the personal data was transmitted, the legal or commercial relationship that exists or existed with the company, your email address or mailing address to notify you of the response to your request, the date of the request, your handwritten signature, and a copy of your official identification. All these requirements are requested to ensure that the request is issued in a verifiable manner concerning the data subject or requester.

Exclusion List.
"REDCYCLE" will have an exclusion list of information, in which all information that the data subject wishes to be excluded from information processing will be placed and stored. To proceed with such storage, it is necessary for you to submit a written request in which you expressly mention your name and the statement "I, (data subject's name), through this letter, request from the legal entity "REDCYCLE" the exclusion of my information related to: (list or specify what type of data and information you wish to exclude from our processing) regarding the processing of my personal data," the approximate date when the personal data were transmitted, the legal or commercial relationship that exists or existed with the company, your email address or address to notify you of the response to your request, the date of the request, handwritten signature, and a copy of your official identification. We inform you that even if you exclude or revoke consent for information processing, it is our obligation to retain all data, when it concerns accounting information for 5 to 10 years as provided in the Federal Fiscal Code in Article 30, and in the case of commercial information, for 10 years as provided in Article 46 of the Commercial Code. After that time period, the information may be deleted and completely destroyed.
